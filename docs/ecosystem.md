# Ecosystem

## Participants

The Jutsu Ecosystem is designed to serve a variety of participants, each playing a crucial role in its development and growth. By focusing on an ecosystem approach, Jutsu ensures that every participant benefits from a collaborative and dynamic environment.

| **Participant** | **Role** | **Benefits** | **Responsibilities** |
|-----------------|----------|--------------|----------------------|
| **Users** | Utilize AI apps for various tasks | Access to a wide variety of AI apps, enhanced productivity, and a user-friendly experience | Participate in the ecosystem, provide feedback and ratings for AI apps |
| **Builders** | Create and deploy AI apps | Access to intuitive no-code tools, monetization opportunities, and community support | Develop high-quality AI apps, collaborate on projects, engage with the community |
| **Curators** | Ensure quality and safety of AI apps | Earn tokens, build reputation, influence platform integrity | Verify and approve AI apps, handle appeals, maintain quality standards |
| **Providers** | Supply AI models and services | Attract new builders, contribute to ecosystem growth, receive feedback for improvement | Provide reliable AI models and services, support integrations, collaborate with Jutsu |

### Users
Users are the end-users who explore and utilize AI apps to enhance productivity and efficiency. They benefit from the diverse offerings within the Jutsu Ecosystem.

### Builders
Builders are the creative force within the Jutsu Ecosystem, responsible for designing and deploying AI apps. They benefit from a range of tools and opportunities that facilitate innovation and monetization.

### Curators
Curators play a vital role in maintaining the quality and safety of AI apps within the ecosystem. They ensure that only high-quality, safe AI apps are accessible to users.

### Providers
Providers supply the AI models and services integrated within the Jutsu Ecosystem. They play a key role in supporting builders and fostering ecosystem growth.

## Core Components

The Jutsu Ecosystem comprises several core components, each integral to its functionality and growth. These components provide the necessary tools, interfaces, and frameworks that enable users and builders to interact, create, and innovate within a collaborative and dynamic environment.

| **Core Component** | **Description** | **Key Features** |
|--------------------|-----------------|------------------|
| **App Hub** | Gateway for end-users to explore and utilize AI apps. | Advanced AI app and task management, search and categorization, and intuitive user-friendly interfaces. |
| **Builder Studio** | Creative core for builders to create, configure, deploy, and monetize AI apps. | Web-based IDE, intelligent tools, API and data integration, multi-agent collaboration, guided workflows, version control. |
| **Marketplace** | Allows builders to publish and monetize AI apps, facilitating reuse and composition of projects. | Publishing and monetization, advanced search, user feedback, app subscription management. |
| **Innovation Portal** | Focuses on fostering innovation, ensuring quality assurance, and implementing dynamic reward systems. | Community-requested projects, qualification criteria, builder participation, verification, quality assurance, appeals, performance rewards. |
| **Community Portal** | Space for platform governance, user participation, and transparency. | Governance participation, user engagement, transparency, educational resources. |

### App Hub
The App Hub is the gateway for end-users to explore and utilize AI apps. It provides a seamless and intuitive interface for discovering, accessing, and managing AI apps tailored to various tasks. Advanced AI app and task management, search and categorization, and intuitive user-friendly interfaces ensure users can efficiently find and use the tools they need to enhance productivity and efficiency.

### Builder Studio
The Builder Studio is the creative core of the Jutsu Ecosystem, offering builders the tools they need to create, configure, deploy, and monetize AI apps. With a web-based integrated development environment, intelligent tools, and robust support for API and data integration, the Builder Studio empowers builders to innovate and bring their AI solutions to life. Multi-agent collaboration features and guided workflows streamline the development process, while version control ensures project integrity and teamwork.

### Marketplace
The AI App Marketplace allows builders to publish and monetize their AI apps, facilitating the reuse and composition of existing projects within the Jutsu Ecosystem. Builders can list their AI apps, set usage fees, and earn revenue based on interactions. The marketplace's advanced search and categorization features help users discover relevant AI apps, while user feedback and ratings ensure quality and reliability. Subscription management options provide flexible access models for users.

### Innovation Portal
The Innovation Portal focuses on quality assurance, dynamic reward systems, and fostering innovation. It enables users to propose bounties for specific projects or AI app improvements, with builders participating to earn rewards. The portal ensures high standards through thorough verification processes and provides a structured environment for developing cutting-edge solutions. By continuously enhancing AI app quality and fostering innovative projects, the Innovation Portal drives forward the capabilities of the Jutsu Ecosystem.

### Community Portal
The Community Portal provides a space for platform governance, user participation, and transparency, fostering a vibrant and engaged community. Users can participate in governance through voting and discussion forums, track contributions via leaderboards, and engage in community events. The portal also offers educational resources, including tutorials, documentation, and workshops, to help users and builders get started and stay engaged. Transparency in governance processes and user contributions ensures accountability and trust within the ecosystem.

## Subscriptions

The Jutsu Ecosystem provides a flexible and scalable subscription model that combines tiered access for users with a revenue-sharing model for builders. This ensures that both users and builders can choose plans that best fit their usage patterns and financial capabilities.

### Users
Users can choose from Free, Personal, and Pro tiers to access AI apps, each offering a different level of features and capabilities.

| **Plan**    | **Description**                                                                 | **Key Features**                                                                                 |
|-------------|---------------------------------------------------------------------------------|--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| **Free**    | The Free tier allows users to explore the Jutsu Ecosystem's basic functionalities without any cost. It is ideal for new users who want to experiment with AI apps. | Limited interaction with a selected number of AI apps, opportunity to explore AI app capabilities, community support. |
| **Personal**| The Personal tier offers additional resources and features for users who require more capabilities than the Free tier. | Increased interaction with a larger selection of AI apps, more extensive usage capabilities, community and email support. |
| **Pro**     | The Pro tier is designed for users with higher resource needs and provides the most extensive access and support. | Pay-per-use model for accessing and interacting with AI apps, subscription management for individual AI apps, enhanced features, priority support. |

#### Free Tier
The Free tier allows users to explore the Jutsu Ecosystem's basic functionalities without any cost, ideal for new users who want to experiment with AI apps. It offers limited interaction with a selected number of AI apps and access to community support.

#### Personal Tier
The Personal tier offers additional resources and features for users who require more capabilities than the Free tier. This includes increased interaction with a larger selection of AI apps and more extensive usage capabilities, supported by community and email assistance.

#### Pro Tier
The Pro tier is designed for users with higher resource needs and provides the most extensive access and support. It includes a pay-per-use model for accessing and interacting with AI apps, subscription management for individual AI apps, enhanced features, and priority support.

### Builders
Builders can sign up and start building immediately without any upfront cost. They benefit from a revenue-sharing model where a fee is taken from their published AI apps based on the subscription model they define for their app.

| **Plan** | **Description**                                                                   | **Key Features**                                                                               |
|----------|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| **Free** | Builders can sign up and start building immediately without any upfront cost. They benefit from a revenue-sharing model where a fee is taken from their published AI apps based on the subscription model they define for their app. | Access to development tools and resources, revenue sharing based on app interactions and subscriptions, transparent systems for fair compensation and recognition. |

Builders have access to a range of development tools and resources, including a web-based integrated development environment, intelligent tools for configuring AI models, and robust support for API and data integration. The Builder Studio empowers builders to innovate and bring their AI solutions to life, with multi-agent collaboration features and guided workflows streamlining the development process. Builders also benefit from version control, ensuring project integrity and teamwork.

Builders earn revenue based on the interactions and subscriptions their AI apps generate, with transparent systems in place to ensure fair compensation and recognition for their contributions. This model encourages continuous innovation and high-quality AI app development within the Jutsu Ecosystem.
